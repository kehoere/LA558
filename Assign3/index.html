Zoos in the USA

Following my passion of zoological design, I chose to learn about the top three zoos in the United States: San Diego Zoo, Lincoln Park Zoo, and St. Louis Zoo. As you can see in the chart and map below, the size of the zoo location depicts how many visitors are attracted to the site, how many animals the site can inhabit, and what major roads are able to access the site. By understanding these features, zoological design can take the next step towards increasing attendees, increasing animal habitats, and increading traffic and awareness in the site location.
